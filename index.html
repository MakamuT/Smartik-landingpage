About Smartik

About

Smartik is a desktop application built for South African educators who handle the high-pressure task of marking Grade 12 (Matric) scripts. Powered by smart technology, it allows you to scan and grade handwritten Matric exam papers quickly and accurately — saving time without compromising quality.

Key Features

  • Scan Handwritten Matric Scripts – Supports PDF scans or camera uploads
  • AI Assisted Grading – Fast and accurate scoring aligned to CAPS standards
  • Real-Time Feedback – Auto-generated feedback for each question
  • Confidential & Secure – Keeps learner data private and protected
  • Built for Teachers – Simple, clean interface made for South African classrooms
